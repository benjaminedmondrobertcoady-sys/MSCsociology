Writer's Workshop SF.5 Mix & Match: The Clause Forge

To learn that the clauses of an analytical sentence can be rearranged, and that what matters is coherence and support for your analysis, not a fixed order.
Worked example

The Clause Forge

Drag a clause to the front to foreground it. Every order stays coherent.

Read for Coherence

Order is free. Coherence is the rule underneath it.
Judge each rearrangement. The question is never whether it followed the pattern, only whether a reader can follow it.

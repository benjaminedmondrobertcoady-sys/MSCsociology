Is it a folkway, or is it a more?

Drag each norm into the box where you think it belongs. Folkways are everyday customs and manners — breaking one makes you seem rude or odd, but not immoral. Mores (say "more-rayz") carry serious moral weight — breaking one makes people think you've done something genuinely wrong. When you're done, hit Reveal & discuss.

Norms to sort
Folkway An everyday custom — breaking it seems rude or strange, not immoral
More A norm with moral weight — breaking it is seen as genuinely wrong

Tip: some of these are genuinely arguable, and the line shifts over time and across cultures — that's the point. Be ready to defend your placement.
